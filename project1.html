Step 4 - Summaries data with use of graphics

By looking at the shared points gathered by individuals when they participate in the quiz, we are able to gauge their cumulative contribution over time. To account for variation in total points awarded each week and the unknown contribution each member may give to the group for each given week, the contribution awarded after each quiz is calculated from the percentile in relation to either the top performing team of that given week. This percentile is then shared evenly between the current participants assuming equal contribution.
